    We know the struggles that refugees face when they escape their country. They have a hard time adjusting to their environment and setting up their new life. At Refugio, we empower refugees by providing them with necessary resources, including education, career, etc., and help them to be financially independent. Click the button below to get started!

    You can access educational and career resources to help you to take control of your life. Education resources are provided to you, for a lower(or free*) price and you will have access to educators, mentors and volunteers. You can also browse for careers and see what suits your interests. Check out the Education and Career tab!

    Feeling lonely? Want to talk to someone who may have underwent the same thing you did? Look no further, Refugio will connect you to other refugees. You can talk to them to voice call or live chat. Clikc the Social tab to get started!
